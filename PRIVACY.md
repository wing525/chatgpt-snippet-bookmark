# Privacy Policy

## Summary
This extension stores your saved snippets locally in your own browser and does not send snippet data to any external server.

## What data is stored
When you save a snippet, the extension may store:
- Selected text
- Page URL and page title
- Timestamp
- Optional tags you add
- Local matching metadata (for re-locating the snippet in the conversation)

## Where data is stored
- `chrome.storage.local` on your device

## What is NOT collected
- No account/password collection
- No analytics/telemetry upload
- No third-party sharing or sale of data

## Network behavior
- The extension does not transmit snippet content to remote APIs or servers.

## Your control
- You can delete individual snippets from the popup.
- You can remove the extension at any time to stop future data collection.
- You can clear extension data from browser extension settings.

## Contact
For questions, open an issue in the project repository.
